FAQ

The blocking doesn't work

Bricked uses Apple's Screen Time (Family Controls). On first launch, allow Screen Time access when asked. Then pick apps and set a limit or schedule — the chosen apps lock when time runs out.

How do I unlock early?

A block is meant to hold. On the free plan you can take a short paid-with-time break; the wait grows with each unlock the same day.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

iPhone → Settings → tap your name → Subscriptions → Bricked. You can also tap “Restore purchases” on the paywall to restore an existing subscription.

Is my data private?

Yes — everything stays on your device. See our Privacy Policy.
