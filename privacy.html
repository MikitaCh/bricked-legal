Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 July 2026

Bricked is designed to keep your data on your device.

What we store

Your blocked apps, limits, schedules, streaks and usage stats are stored only on your device (and its private iCloud app-group container). We do not run analytics servers and this data never leaves your phone.

Screen Time

Bricked uses Apple’s Family Controls / Screen Time APIs to block apps on your device. Your app-usage details are handled locally by iOS; Bricked cannot read your browsing history or personal content.

Purchases

Subscriptions are processed by Apple and by RevenueCat, which manages subscription status. RevenueCat receives a random, non-identifying app user ID and purchase/receipt information to keep your subscription in sync. See RevenueCat’s privacy policy at revenuecat.com/privacy.

No tracking

Bricked contains no ads, no third-party trackers, and does not sell any data.
